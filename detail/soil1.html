Moisture Sensor



  • Usually used to detect amount of moisture of the soil.


  • Uses capacitance to measure dielectric permittivity of the surrounding medium


  • Aid good irrigation management.


  • Watermark sensors offer a larger moisture reading range from 0 to 200 cb or kpa.


  • It delivers the results immediately.
