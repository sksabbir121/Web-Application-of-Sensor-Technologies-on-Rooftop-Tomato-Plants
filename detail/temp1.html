Temperature and Humidity Sensor

  • Low-cost digital temperature and humidity sensor.


  • uses a capacitive humidity sensor and a thermistor to measure the surrounding air, and spits out a digital signal


  • Humidity is the measure of water vapour present in the air.


  • This sensor can be easily interfaced with any micro-controller such as Arduino, Raspberry Pi etc and to measure humidity and temperature instantaneously.


  • The DHT sensors are made of two parts, a capacitive humidity sensor and a thermistor. There is also a very basic chip inside that does some analog to digital conversion and spits out a digital signal with the temperature and humidity.
